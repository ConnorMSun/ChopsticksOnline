Chopsticks Online!

Chopsticks is a short and sweet game where two players are given a set of 'hands' with a value of one, then take turns adding a hand's to another of the three hands. If a hand ever reaches a value above 4, a value of 5 is subtracted from that hand. To win, you must get your opponent to have both of their hands at value 0!

Friendly competion can boost motivation, stimulate personal growth, and help you destress! Chopsticks Online a great way to participate in low-stakes competition with your friends, family, or even strangers!
